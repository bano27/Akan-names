Akan Names

This application was developed to help those seeking on naming their kin according to the Ghanian culture.

It also is of assist to those curious on learning the Ghanian culture and naming ceremonies play a big part in the culture hence helps more people interact and embrace the culture

According to the ghanian culture they used days of the week as reference of what name a child either male or female would be named if they were to be born on a certain day of the week

Below is a table that summarizes what type of name one would get during such ceremonies;

Day of week Male Female
Sunday Kwasi Akosua
Monday Kwadwo Adwoa
Tuesday Kwabena Abenaa
Wednesday Kwaku Akua
Thursday Yaw Yaa
Friday Kofi Afua
Saturday Kwame Ama
